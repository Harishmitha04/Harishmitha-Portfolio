IRCTC UX/UI Redesign

A UX Case Study to Simplify Train Booking in India

#UXResearch #Redesign #UIUX #CaseStudy

🔍 The Problem

IRCTC’s existing app and website are widely used but often criticized for being cluttered, slow, and unintuitive. The goal was to improve usability, aesthetics, and task flow efficiency.

🎯 My Role

  • UX Research
  • Wireframing & Prototyping
  • User Testing
  • UI Design in Figma

🧠 Research & Insights

I conducted user interviews and analyzed over 50 user reviews from the Play Store. Common complaints included confusing navigation, too many steps to book tickets, and outdated visuals.

✏️ Wireframes

🎨 Final UI Design

Using Figma, I redesigned the interface with modern aesthetics, improved hierarchy, better spacing, and a simplified booking flow.

📈 Results & Impact

After testing with 10 users, 90% found the redesigned interface easier to use, and task completion time dropped by 40%.
